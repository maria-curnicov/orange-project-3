Preparation

    Making the Pizza Dough
  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
    Preparing the Pizzas
  1. Preheat the pizza stone (or pizza pan or baking sheet)
  2. Prep the toppings
  3. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  4. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  5. Sprinkle the pizza peel with cornmeal, put flattened dough on top
  6. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  7. Slide pizza into the oven
  8. Bake
